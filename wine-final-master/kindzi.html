KINDZMARAULI MARANI

Kindzmarauli is one of the most well-known Georgian semi-sweet wines given its popularity in the Soviet period. Kindzmarauli is sweet but not too sweet because it is made from a tannic and complex grape varietal, Saperavi. The characteristics of the Saperavi grape lend balance to the sugar resulting in a well structured wine great for pairings.

Khikhvi is ancient Georgian specien of grape. Historically motherland of Khikhvi is Kardenakhi. Wine made from Khikhvi stands out with variety aroma and race of wine, inwhich strong tones of flowers and druit can be felt.

Dark red with twinkling hints. Complex aromas of aged Saperavi with loads of red and black fruit with scent of smoke and spices. Nicely structured, supple first taste is dominated by blackberry. Ripe tannins are in right intensity defining the lingering finish in combination with fruit flavors.
