USAKHELOURI

Natural semi-sweet red wine is made from the variety local red grapes named USAKHELOURI, spread in Lechkhumi, mainly in the micro zone of Zubi-Okureshi. The wine is characterized with beautiful ruby color or ripe promegrante color, having special aromas, pleasant sweet flawor and harmonius minute of compaunds. The grapes are harvested from the company vineyards, located in the village Zubi.
