Ojaleshi is a medium-sweet red wine produced from indigenous olajeshi grapes. It's light red in color, shows a delicate herbaceous bouquet, with notes of raspberry, redcurrant, and red rose. The taste is well balanced between the fresh acidity and natural sweetness. Matches with desserts, sweet-sour fruit salads, or soft cheese.

Dry red wine with a characteristic dense dark garnet color, ripe red berries in the aroma harmoniously matches with the rich flavor and velvety wine, in which the natural sweetness and tartness are balanced by pleasant acidity.

Tvishi is an appellation of origin and this wine is made of Tsolikouri grapes, making it one of the most distinctive Georgian white semi-sweet wines. The specific zone called Tvishi is located in the region of Racha, on the right shore of Rioni river, at an altitude of 435 meters above sea level.
