KHUTSISHVILI BROTHERS

Kisi is white semi-sweet wine. It is unique, rare species of Georgian grapes, local variety of Kakheti. It was less common in Soviet times and that’s why it is not wide spread. The Kisi wine has dark straw color when it is made by Kakhetian method. It wine is distinguished by aroma. It’s tones are close to citrus, mango and orange tones.

It is dry white wine. A bouquet of floral aromas thanks to the Mtsvane, good acidity thanks to Rkatsiteli and lighter softer tannin on the finish due to less time on the skins. Delicious to explore with food.

It is dry white wine. Wine is made in Napareuli microzone from the best grapes of Rkatsiteli species picked in Gamtkitsulashvili brothers’ vineyards. It is made by fermentation in Qvevri based on family traditions. The wine is distinguished by the abundance of Rkatsiteli and Kakhetian Green species and the harmonious taste because of Qvevri, also by the gentle bouquet that is characteristic of Napareuli microzone.

Saperavi Is red dry wine. It is made in Napareuli microzone from the best grapes of Saperavi species picked in Gamtkitsulashvili brothers’ vineyards. It is made by fermentation in Qvevri based on family traditions. It has an elegant dryness and tasteful features, the abundance of Saperavi species and the harmonious taste that is a characteristic of Qvevri.
