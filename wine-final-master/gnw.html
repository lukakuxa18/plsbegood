GNW

White dry wine. Made from a Chinese grape variety cultivated in the village of Arbo (Eredvi Micro Zone) in Shida Kartli, which is an Aboriginal and a priority grape variety in this region. It is characterized by purple color, apricot, ripe peach and tropical fruit aroma.

100% tavkveri. One of the famous endemic varieties of Georgia from which harmonious red and cheerful pink wines are produced.
