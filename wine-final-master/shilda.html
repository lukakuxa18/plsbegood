Red Semi-sweet. Akhasheni is denomination of origin wine, produced from Saperavi grape variety cultivated in strictly controlled Akhasheni micro-zone, Kakheti region. The wine has ruby color and famed for its characteristic taste, with the tones of red fruits and berries. Has a pleasant, harmonious taste with soft tannins and a long aftertaste. Serve as aperitif, with fruits and desert.

Dark rich cherry color. Mulberry and sweet cherry aromas are entiched with aged oak bouquet and spicy tones. Rich but velvety tannin texture with balanced acidity. It is red dry wine.

White Dry. Kisi is table wine produced from Kisi grape variety cultivated in Kakheti region. The wine has light amber color, well defined varietal aroma with the tones of wild flowers, harmonious taste, live, soft tannins and long aftertaste. Serve with fish, seafood, vegetable salads and cheese.
