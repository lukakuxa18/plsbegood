Red semi-dry wine. "Pirosmani" wine has an intense ruby colour with violet hue, ripe blackberry and blackcurrant with the hints of prune. The wine is young and full-bodied, with dense sweet fruits, soft yet spicy with well balanced blackberry finish.

White semi-sweet wine. Alazani Valley-Table white semi – sweet wine made from Rqatsiteli grape grown in the Alazani Valley in Kakheti. It has a light straw colour and harmoniously velvety sweetness of fruits. It is an irreplaceable drink for the celebrations.

Red semi-sweet wine. Alazani Valley-MEDIUM SWEET RED WINE . Manufacttured since 1977 from the grapes of the Saperavi, grown in the Alazani Valley in Kakheti. The wine has a drink red colour and full velvety taste.

Red semi-sweet wine. Kindzmarauli is one of the most famous Georgian wines. It is made grapes of Saperavi, cultivated in Kakheti, in the protected micro – zone of Kindzmarauli. It has a strong sorted bouquet and aroma together with velvety and harmonious taste.

Dry white wine. Tsinandali, dry white wine, is made in Kakheti, in East Georgia. The wine is prepared by Rkastiteli and Mtsvane grape varieties. It has light maize yellow tint and vivid, soft harmonic taste.

Dry red wine. The wine is made from Saperavi grape variety cultivated in Kakheti in the Mukuzani micro region, where the winemaking tradition stretches back to antiquity. Mukuzani is the color of dark ruby, with a fine fruty bouquet and full, fresh and harmonious taste. It has manufactured since 1888.
