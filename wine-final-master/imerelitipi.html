Dry red wine is made from the pitcher of the Otskhani Saperian grape variety, grown in Imereti, western Georgia. The wine has a dark pomegranate color, characterized by aromas of rosemary, berries and spices. Tastes a lively, bodily taste, with an acidity and a long finish. The crop is harvested grain-by-grain. Taste at + 16-18 ° with a spicy dish, well blended with meat, raw herbs and vegetables.
