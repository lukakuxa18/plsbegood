LOLADZE

White dry wine. 2017 harvest of village manavi. In the Kakheti region of Georgia. grapes are hand-picked at the optimum of their maturity. Pressed withhout stalks. After 3 days of maceration was removed from the pomace. Due to no technological processing can put a small pellet.

Dry red wine. 2018 harvest of village Napareuli. In the Kakheti region of Georgia. Grapes are hand-picked at the optimum of their marurity. Pressed without stalks. After 9 days of maceration was removed from the pomace. Due to no technological processing can put a small pellet
